What is the right cage size for my bird?

Choosing the right cage size is crucial for your bird's physical and mental well-being. A cramped cage can lead to stress, feather plucking, and other health problems. As a general rule, the cage should be large enough for your bird to spread its wings fully and fly comfortably between perches. Here are some resources to help you determine the appropriate size for your specific bird species: * https://louisville.edu/research/iacuc/policy-files/RRFCagingGuide.pdf * https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=WnDLvS2T4eI Remember, bigger is usually better! If you have space, consider opting for a larger cage than the minimum recommended size.
